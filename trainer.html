TrainerTracker

A web application that connects trainers and thier trainees through a clean and simple user interface.

RedwoodJS
ReactJS
GraphQL
Prisma
ChartJS
Netlify Identity

TrainerTracker gives trainers and trainees an platform to assign and plan workouts remotely. With the ability to abilityt to add and select clients trainers are able to create and edit workouts for their clients that will be deisplayed on a bar graph to make tracking workout progress fun and easy. Trainees have the ability to log their workouts and view their progress against what their trainers have assigned for them. Both trainers and trainers can use the exercise tracker to select and view total progress over time for each exercise.
